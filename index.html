Advancing methods, processes, and frameworks in UX UI design

The current research topic explores how to advance user-centered design (UCD) methods by investigating the potential implications of AI in design thinking, seeking ways to mitigate implicit bias in personas, and addressing a lack of built-in inclusivity in the UCD approach. The discoveries will help UX design practitioners and educators elevate the learning and design outcomes.
